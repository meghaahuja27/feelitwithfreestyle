My Story

Hi, I'm Meg!

I am a professional freestyle dancer who has been running workshops where I guide people through the art of freestyle movement. For me, dance isn't a skill to master — it's a form of expression. A universal language that connects people from all over the world.

My work has been highlighted by a Kpop agency and I've been part of dance battles — but what truly drives me is helping others discover what movement can do for their soul.

"Movement is the language your soul has always known."
